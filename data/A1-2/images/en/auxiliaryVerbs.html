1. Transitive verbs (verbs that can answer the question "whom?" / "what?"):
machen – to do, schreiben – to write, wissen – to know, lesen – to read. 		1. Intransitive verbs (verbs that cannot answer "whom?" / "what?"), expressing movement or a change of state:
gehen – to go, fliegen – to fly, sterben – to die, einschlafen – to fall asleep.
2. Intransitive verbs that are not verbs of movement or change of state:
stehen – to stand, sitzen – to sit, liegen – to lie, schlafen – to sleep. 		2. Verbs:
sein – to be, werden – to become, bleiben – to remain, passieren – to happen.
3. Reflexive verbs (with sich):
sich waschen – to wash oneself, sich ärgern – to get angry, sich interessieren – to be interested.
