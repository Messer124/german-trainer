Regular verbs
Form General question Imperativ
du (you) Machst du? Mach(e)! Remove the verb ending and pronoun; sometimes add -e.
ihr (you, plural) Macht ihr? Macht! Only the pronoun is removed.
Sie (you, formal) Machen Sie? Machen Sie! Only the question mark is removed.
wir (we) Machen wir? Machen wir! Only the question mark is removed.
