The verb lassen: additional meanings (when used as an auxiliary-like verb)

a. leave something unchanged

Immer lässt er sein Handy zu Hause liegen. — He always leaves his phone at home.

Sie lässt nachts das Licht brennen. — At night she leaves the light on.

b. have someone do something, not do it yourself

Ich lasse meine Haare schneiden. — I am having my hair cut.
Literally: I have someone cut my hair.

c. allow / let someone do something

Sie lässt ihr Kind den ganzen Tag spielen. — She lets her child play all day.

Lass mich gehen! — Let me go!

d. lassen + sich as a substitute for passive with the modal verb können

Viele Krankheiten lassen sich heilen. — Many illnesses can be cured (literally: allow themselves to be cured).

Das Gerät lässt sich nicht reparieren. — The device cannot be repaired (it does not allow itself to be repaired).

e. urge or invite someone to act

Lassen wir uns nach Salzburg fahren! — Let's go to Salzburg!
