Rule TeKaMoLo

German sentences can be expanded with four types of information:

  • Temporal (time-related information), from Latin tempus — time (When?);
  • Causal — information about reason: why something happens (Why?);
  • Modal — describes the way or manner (How?);
  • Local — information about place (Where? To where? From where?).
Who/what? What does he/she/it do? When? Why? How? Where?
Ich bin gestern wegen der Prüfung mit dem Auto in die Uni gefahren.
Markus fährt morgen aus Liebe schnell nach Berlin.
