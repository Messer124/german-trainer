The passive voice with modal verbs

Very often we need to show that a passive action, for example, must or can be carried out. In this case, modal verbs are used:

müssen Das Haus muss in Kürze gebaut werden. — The house must be built soon.
können Das Haus kann in 3 Monaten gebaut werden. — The house can be built in three months.
wollen Ich will morgen gefragt werden. — I want to be asked tomorrow.
möchten Er möchte vom Lehrer gelobt werden. — He would like to be praised by the teacher.
sollen Er soll gelobt werden. — He should be praised.
dürfen Er darf gelobt werden. — He may be praised.

The modal verb takes the second position in the sentence and is conjugated, while the main verb in Partizip II moves to the end and stands before werden.
