III. Zero article (noun without an article); pronoun viele (plural); numerals (eins, zwei, drei)

Masculine Neuter Feminine Plural
Nominative großer Mann kleines Kind schöne Frau gute Kinder
Accusative großen Mann kleines Kind schöne Frau gute Kinder
Dative großem Mann kleinem Kind schöner Frau guten Kindern
Genitive großen* Mannes kleinen* Kindes schöner Frau guter Kinder

*Even though in Genitive (masculine and neuter) the definite article is “des” and the adjective ending with a zero article should also be “es”, this does not happen and the ending remains “en”.

  • Kleine Kinder mögen schönes Wetter. — Small children like nice weather.
  • Viele kleine Kinder wollen helfen. — Many small children want to help.
  • Drei kleine Kinder haben uns geholfen. — Three small children helped us.
