Plusquamperfekt is used to coordinate two actions that both happened in the past, but one of them was completed earlier.

Typical usage patterns:

With the conjunction als

Als ich nach Hause gekommen bin, hatte er den Anruf bereits beendet. — When I came home, he had already finished the call.
First he finished the call, then I arrived.

With the conjunction nachdem

Nachdem ich nach Hause gekommen war, hat er den Anruf beendet. — After I had come home, he finished the call.
First I came home, then he finished the call.

With the conjunction bevor

Bevor ich nach Hause gekommen bin, hatte er den Anruf beendet. — Before I came home, he had finished the call.
First he finished the call, then I arrived.

In any other situation where two actions happened in the past.

Ich hatte das Buch gelesen, danach habe ich Tee getrunken. — I had read the book, then I drank tea.

Ich hatte gerade mein Frühstück beendet, da klingelte das Telefon. — I had just finished my breakfast when the phone rang.
