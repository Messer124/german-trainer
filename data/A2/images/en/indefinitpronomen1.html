Nouns can be replaced by the pronouns einer / keiner / welche when it is clear from the previous context which noun is meant.

einer
Used only in the singular; declines by gender and case

Hast du ein Buch? — Ja, ich habe eins. Do you have a book? — Yes, I have one.

Ist das ein MP3-Player? — Ja, das ist einer. Is that an MP3 player? — Yes, it is one.

keiner
Used in both singular and plural; declines by gender and case

Hast du ein Buch? — Nein, ich habe keins. Do you have a book? — No, I don't have one.

Hast du noch Äpfel? — Nein, ich habe keine. Do you still have apples? — No, I don't have any.

welche
Used in the plural and with uncountable nouns; declines by gender and case

Hast du noch Äpfel? — Ja, ich habe noch welche. Do you still have apples? — Yes, I still have some.

Ich brauche Milch, hast du welche? I need milk, do you have any?
